1. Rain water percolates into the soil 
3. Soil *CO_{2}* diffuses and forms carbonic acid
4. *H_{2}O + CO_{2} \Leftrightarrow{} H_{2}CO_{3} \Leftrightarrow{} CO_{3}^{2-} + H^{+}*
5. Since *HO_{2}* >>> *CO_{2}* \there4{} *\delta{}\shy{}^{18}O_{water}* \tilde{} *\delta{}\shy{}^{18}O_{air}*
6. *CaCO_3 + 2H^{+ } \Rightarrow{} Ca^{2+} + H_{2}O + CO_{2}*
7. Liquid enters a void, *CO_{2}* degasses
8. *CaCO_3* precipitates onto the surface (easier than in solution - *nucleation potential* so no mud)
\\
\\
... Then used to interpret paleoclimate
